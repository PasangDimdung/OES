Instructions

Before you begin your exam

1. Make sure you have good and stable internet connection.

2. Disconnect all the connected devices from the wifi or use ethernet cable if possible.

3. Close all the applications running in the background.

4. Do not open any other tabs on the browser apart from the exam tab.

5. Maximize your browser window before starting the exam.


During the exam

1. Do not resize or minimize the browser.

2. Do not close exam tab or refresh the browser before your exam is completed.

3. Don't close the exam tab even if you face the connection problem because it prevents our system from tracking your selected answer.

4. Keep an eye on timer clock to keep track of the time left for the examination.

If you encounter any problem during examination, you can contact your college immediately.
